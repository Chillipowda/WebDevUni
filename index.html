Who We Are

Ice Nine Kills, a metalcore band formed from Boston, Massachusetts. Originally formed in 2002. We work with fearless records to bring you the music you deserve.

What We Are

As a band, our main inspiration is infusing horror within our music. Many references from some of our most popular songs include - amongst others - Jason Voorhees and Jekyll & Hyde.
